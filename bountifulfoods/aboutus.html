Rocky soil makes the plant grow better

When starting our company back in 1989 we wanted to make sure our customers felt how much we love our fruit and how much of that love gets to be experienced by them because of the quality of our fruit. Now we don't have someone go read our plants a bed time story every night but we do treat them like our children.

We hope you enjoy our smoothies! We strive for the highest quality of fruit to show off to the world.
